Budget execution, operational variance, and segment performance presented in a board-ready monitoring format.
Method, scope and comparability notes

Revenue is assessed against the approved MoF budget dated 22 Feb 2025. Zone-level budgets use IPSAS 18 proportional allocation, so they support segment comparison rather than direct zone budget accountability.

Cost analysis is restricted to field-level operations captured in the database. HQ overhead, depreciation, and finance costs remain outside scope. Statistical process control uses an ISO 7870-2 Shewhart X-bar approach built from 11 months of actuals.

Tariff is treated as fixed at MK 1,450/m³, so revenue variance is interpreted primarily as a volume effect. BPI = Actual ÷ Budget; values above 1.0 are favourable for revenue and volume metrics.
